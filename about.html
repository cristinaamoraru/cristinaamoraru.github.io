About Socially Savvy Apps

Research shows that children and teenagers on the autism spectrum can be highly motivated by interventions delivered with an iPad.

If you have a child or teenager on the autism spectrum, and you struggle to keep them motivated while explaining social rules and behaviours to them, you could consider iPad apps as a learning support.

Socially Savvy Apps finds and reviews iPad apps that teach social skills to children and adolescents on the autism spectrum, so that you can choose between these apps easily.

Subscribe to the Socially Savvy Apps Youtube channel and get the latest reviews straight in your feed.

If you find the videos helpful, please use the links in the video descriptions to download the apps (even for the free apps).
